Bikes & Mopeds
Across the island, bikes and mopeds are main modes of transportation for residents and visitors alike. Both bikes and mopeds can be rented for an hour-long trip through town or a full day of exploring the island.

Water Sports

From kayaking across a pond to sailing the open ocean, Block Island offers a wide range of boating options. If you're feeling adventurous, try parasailing, jet skiing, water skiing, and more! Divers can explore centuries-old shipwrecks and embark on shark cage trips. For those who want to stay close to shore, the island plenty of hotspots for snorkelikng, like Surf Beach, and Black Rock Beach for skilled surfers. And be sure to take advantage of ponds and bays across the island for paddleboarding and fishing.

Adventures on Land

There's plenty to do on the island, like hiking and horeback riding. Block Island is known for its pictureque trails, and has something for everyone, from a leisurely beach stroll to a steep hike. Mohegan Bluffs, featuring over 140 steps down the cliffs leading to the water, is a must-see. For a more peaceful walk, try Clayhead Trail, leading to quiet beaches. Or, explore the Corn Maze for a walk the kids will love. For an exciting activity for the whole family, enjoy the beach on horeback with a sunset ride.

R&R

If you're looking for a relaxing retreat, take advantage of Block Island's salons, spas, yoga studios. Koru Eco Spa, at the center of town, is a one-stop shop for hair, nails, massages, facials and more. For yoga and barre classes for all ages and ability levels, try Elevation Studio on Spring Street.
