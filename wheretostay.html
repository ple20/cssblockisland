Rentals
Block Island offers countless rental options, from secluded cottages to oceanside estates. Rentals are available by the day, week, or month.

Hotels and Inns

If you don't need a whole house, you may opt for a hotel or inn.

When you arrive at Old Harbor by ferry, the Harborside Inn might be the first thing to catch your eye as you approach. In addition to being an island landmark, it boasts 36 guest rooms, plus waterfront restaurant The Harbor Grill.

For those seeking luxury and privacy, The 1661 is a top choice - it features 9 guest roons and 7 nearby cottages, and is open year-round. Enjoy a champagne brunch and complimentary afternoon hors d'oevres, and explore the nearby farm and garden.

The Spring House Hotel is another island favorite. In its 160-plus years, the hotel has hosted guests such as Billy Joel and Mark Twain. It boasts a restaurant serving lunch and dinner plus 33 guedt rooms, and is situated on an expansive lawn overlooking the water.
