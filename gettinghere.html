Plane

Air service into Block Island Airport (BID) is offered daily, year-round. from Westerly, RI via New england Airlines. Flight time is 12 minutes. Charter flights through the airline can also be arranged from airports throughout New England. BID is also home to Bethany's Airport Diner, a popular spot for breakfast and lunch.

Boat / Harbor

Block Island is home to two harbors: New Harbor, on the east side of the island, and Old Harbor, on the west side of the island, in the center of town. Both offer space for anchoring on a first come first serve basis.
